Our Menu

We’ve selected the best coffee recipes to suit every taste and strive to bring coffee culture to a new level.

Americano

Espresso diluted with hot water – balanced taste, perfect for a long, calm break.

Espresso

A concentrated shot of coffee – intense and aromatic, the foundation of all espresso-based drinks.

Doppio

A double espresso – double energy and rich flavor for true coffee lovers.

Cappuccino

Espresso, steamed milk, and dense foam – the perfect mix of creaminess and aroma.

Latte

A smooth drink with more milk and less espresso – ideal for those who prefer a mild taste.

Flat White

Double espresso with microfoam milk – velvety texture, rich but balanced flavor.

Mocha

Espresso mixed with chocolate and milk – smooth and perfectly balanced.

Affogato

A refined Italian dessert – a scoop of vanilla ice cream “drowned” in a hot espresso shot.

Caramel Latte

Smooth espresso with steamed milk and a hint of caramel syrup for a sweet, silky flavor.

Irish Coffee

Strong coffee mixed with Irish whiskey, brown sugar, and cream – a timeless classic full of character.

Cortado

Espresso mixed with an equal amount of warm milk – smooth and well-balanced.

Macchiato

A strong espresso “stained” with a touch of milk foam – simple yet powerful in flavor.

Iced Latte

Refreshing espresso combined with cold milk and ice cubes – a summer favorite.

Iced Mocha

Chilled coffee, milk, and creamy chocolate over ice – a sweet blend of dessert and energy.

Iced Caramel Macchiato

Layers of espresso, cold milk, and caramel syrup – silky texture and rich sweetness.

Cold Brew

Slowly infused in cold water for hours – smooth, low-acid, and naturally aromatic.

Frappuccino

A delicious blend of coffee, milk, and ice, topped with whipped cream – a favorite cold dessert drink.

Iced Vanilla Latte

Cold espresso with vanilla-flavored milk – sweet, refreshing, and perfectly balanced.

About Us

At Good Coffee, we believe every day starts better with a good cup of coffee.
We opened our café in the heart of Ungheni to bring an authentic, simple, and sincere experience — a moment of flavor and calm in the middle of daily rush.

We carefully select our coffee beans, freshly roasted and brewed with passion by our baristas.
Every espresso, latte, or cappuccino tells a small story — about time, tradition, and the joy of savoring the moment.

At Good Coffee, it’s not just about drinks — it’s about people.
About the smiles of those who come for the first time and return for the unmistakable taste and our warm atmosphere.
